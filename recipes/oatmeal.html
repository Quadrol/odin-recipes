Basic Oatmeal Recipe

A hot bowl of Oatmeal!

Oatmeal is a quick and easy breakfast when you want something warm but don't want to slave over a frying pan cooking eggs and bacon.

Ingredients

  • Oats (quick oats or regular)
  • Water
  • (Optional)Toppings
    • Fruit such as blueberries or strawberries
    • Cinnamon
    • Brown Sugar
    • Peanut Butter
    • Or any other sweet topping!

Steps

  1. Following directions for purchased oats, add water to pot.
  2. Add oats and bring to a low boil.
  3. Reduce heat and cook for time according to directions. *Note* Quick oats as suggested, will cook faster!
  4. Check if oatmeal is cooked, additional time might be requierd
  5. Scoop portion of oatmeal into bowl
  6. (Optional)Add toppings! Add ontop or stir in!
