Basic Pasta Recipe

A basic pasta dish with red sauce

Pasta and sauce is an easy to make meal with ingredients easily stored in the pantry for a rainy day! One pot and a stove burner is all you need to feed yourself or your family!

Ingredients

  • Dried Pasta
  • 1 Cup Red Pasta Sauce
  • (Optional) 1/2 Cup Shredded or Grated cCeese
    • Parmesan
    • Cheddar
    • Gouda
    • Or your favorite cheese!

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, add a pinch of salt and dried pasta to water.
  3. Follow cooking time for pasta found on box.
  4. Strain pasta and reduce heat to low.
  5. Return pasta to heat and quicly add sauce. Stir gently till sauce is warmed.
  6. (Optional)After plating, top with cheese of choice!
