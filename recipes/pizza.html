Basic Pizza Recipe

A simple cheese pizza recipe ready for your favorite toppings

This basic cheese pizza recipe on its own is already delicious, but can easily be taken to the next level with your favorite toppings! The base recipe also calls for a standard red pizza sauce but even this could be replaced with an alfredo sauce or pesto or even a light drizzle of olive oil!

Ingredients

  • Store bought pizza dough
  • 1 Cup Red Pizza Sauce
  • 2 Cups Shredded cheese
    • Mozarella
    • Cheddar
    • Fontina
    • Or your favorite cheese!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degreed Farenheit.
  2. *Note* If pizza dough is frozen, thaw in fridge overnight. Put parchment paper on a sheet pan and flatten out the pizza dough trying to keep a uniform thickness. Work the dough out from the center towards the edges. Your pizza can be round or square!
  3. Spread a thin layer of sauce ontop of the dough, leaving some room around the edger for a crust to form.
  4. Sprinkle your cheese of choice ontop of the sauce. It's ok for some cheese to fall on the crust, it will crisp up and make the crust extra tasty!
  5. (Optional)Sprinklke additional toppings ontop of cheese. Toppings can also be layered under or between cheese layers!
  6. Bake on sheetpan untill cheese is melted and browns a little. Time will very depending on thickness of crust.
